Introduction

Executive Destiny Productions, LLC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit executivedestinyproductions.org (the "Site").

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address when you submit a form on our Site.
  • Business Information: Company name, order volume, and product preferences when you submit a wholesale or government inquiry.
